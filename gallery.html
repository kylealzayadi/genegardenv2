Our operating hours are Monday through Friday, from 12 PM to 1 PM, and we consistently ensure our availability during these times.
To access our services, simply provide your name, that's all we require. There's no need for an address, age, or any form of documentation. Our approach is minimalist, and we ask for your name solely to address you personally.
Although we don't offer housing services directly, there are other programs in Hollywood that can assist, such as the Salvation Army or the Center, available Monday through Friday.
Feel free to approach any member of our team with your questions. If we don't have the answers immediately, we'll make inquiries on your behalf. Rest assured, we're committed to providing you with the most satisfactory responses to meet your needs. Or call us here: +1 (323) 500-2766.
