About Us

Welcome to Genevieves Garden Hollywood, Since 2020

Genevieve's Garden, established by Brother Henry in 2020, is more than a meal provider for the community; it's a sanctuary for those in need, including the homeless and low-income individuals.

Under the leadership of JL, the organization extends its mission to offer comprehensive support and assistance. JL is known for his approachability and readiness to help, embodying the garden's ethos of community care and support.
